ISS Facts

  • The ISS travels at 5 miles a second, circling the planet every 90 minutes.
  • It's the third brightest object in the sky after the moon and Venus.
  • It cost over $120 billion to build and assemble.
  • The pee from both the astronauts and onboard animals is captured and filtered to be re-used as drinking water.
  • In 2014, 4 separate spacecraft docked with the station.
